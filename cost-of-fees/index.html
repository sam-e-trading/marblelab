How to use it

  1. Enter your average trade size and monthly trade count.
  2. Estimate spread or slippage as a percentage of notional. Keep it honest; tiny assumptions compound too.
  3. Add commission, swap, and platform costs that apply to your setup.
  4. Compare the gross ending balance with the net balance, then check how much gross profit the fees consumed.
