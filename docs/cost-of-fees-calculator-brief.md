# Cost of Fees Calculator Brief

## Purpose

Show traders the difference between a gross return assumption and the net account path after recurring trading costs.

## Scope

- Inputs: starting balance, average trade notional, trades per month, spread/slippage percentage, commission amount and basis, swap/overnight monthly cost, platform/account fee, gross monthly return, and time horizon.
- Outputs: monthly fee drag, annualised drag against starting balance, gross ending balance, net ending balance, total fees paid, gross profit, net profit, and percentage of gross profit consumed by fees.
- Visuals: gross vs net projection chart, monthly cost breakdown chart, and a compact projection table.
- Presets: low-cost, active trader, and overtrading goblin.

## Notes

This is public education, not financial advice. The model intentionally uses constant monthly activity and fee assumptions so the effect is easy to understand.
