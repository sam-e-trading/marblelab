Important assumptions

  1. Fixed-risk v1: this tool uses your entered dollar value as 1R for every closed position in the statement.
  2. Stop loss is diagnostic only: moved, breakeven, missing, or non-adverse stops are flagged but never used as the source of R.
  3. Future Phase 2: a later MT5 EA or algo export could record max gross loss, original per-trade risk, MAE/MFE, and intended 1R so users can upload that alongside the statement for more accurate per-trade R.
  4. Educational use only: outputs are for trade review and learning, not financial advice or a recommendation to trade.
